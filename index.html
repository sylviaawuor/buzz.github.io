ABOUT

Crystal Rivers is located 25Km out of Nairobi CBD and 1.2Km past the Athi River Interchange. Far enough to escape the daily hustle and bustle, yet still close enough to offer an easy commute to the city when required.

A 10-minute drive away from the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport and 20 minutes to CBD by rail, its location along the Mombasa Road dual carriageway and close proximity to upcoming Konza City, is a great addition to the emerging residential and business needs of the greater Nairobi Metropolis.

Every day at Crystal Rivers is an opportunity to enjoy a buzz-filled, fun lifestyle experience for mom, dad and kids. You will be spoilt for choice between spending the day at our spacious, exciting Mall and relaxing in the comfort of your own home at Crystal Rivers.

Crystal Rivers comprises of a state-of-the-art mall that covers 5.7 acres with a total of 200,000 Sq Ft. of lettable area, and a residential area consisting of 3 and 4 bedroom townhouses, plus a cluster of 3 bedroom apartments.
